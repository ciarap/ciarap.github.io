Travel Information

Waterford is very accessible as there are many motorways and train routes running through the county. Main links between Waterford and counties such as Dublin,Cork,Limerick and Galway are available.
Main airports in Dublin and Cork have bus services running daily to Waterford city. An airport is located in Waterford also, but has limited flight routes.

Waterford is within easy reach of Dublin, Cork and Rosslare Europort.

Main Routes to Waterford

By Car to Waterford City
From Dublin: approx 1 hour 50 minutes via the M9 (motorway all the way)
From Cork: approx 1 hour 40 minutes via the N25
From Shannon: approx 2 hours 30 minutes via N24
From Kilkenny: approx 35 minutes via M9
Download full directions from the AA website

By Car to Dungarvan (West Waterford)
From Dublin: approx 2 hours 30 minutes via M9 and N25
From Cork: approx 1 hour via N25
From Shannon: approx 3 hours via N24
From Kilkenny: approx 1 hour 10 minutes via M9 and N25
Download full directions from the AA website

By Train
It's possible to take a train from Dublin to Waterford City. Timetables are available at www.irishrail.ie

By Bus
Information on buses to Waterford City, Tramore and Dungarvan can be found at www.buseireann.ie or www.jjkavanagh.ie
