Accommodation

There are many beautiful hotels located throughout County Waterford. Some of which are shown below.

Granville Hotel

The Granville Hotel Waterford overlooks the waterfront and marina in the heart of Waterford City’s business and shopping centre. The Granville hotel in Waterford offers 100 comfortable guestrooms, extensive conference, banqueting and Waterford Wedding venue facilities along with the award winning Bianconi restaurant and Thomas Francis Meagher bar.

The Granville Hotel Waterford is one of the best located hotels in Waterford, situated in the centre of Waterford City. The luxury 4 star hotel is only minutes from Waterford’s Regional Airport, bus and train stations and within easy reach of the world famous Waterford Crystal visitor centre, Waterford City Walking Tours, Reginald’s Tower, Bishop's Palace, Waterford's Viking Triangle, Waterford Business Park and Waterford Institute of Technology.

The Granville City centre hotel Waterford offers all that is best in traditional style and grandeur. Justly proud of the Granville’s heritage, the Cusack family, owners since the late seventies, today vigorously pursue the hotels long tradition of hospitality, friendliness and comfort.

From the moment you walk through the doors of the Granville Hotel in Waterford city centre you will experience the professionalism and hospitality that is the essence of the hotel.

A dedicated team of management and staff are on hand to ensure that your stay in Waterford is a pleasant and memorable one at one of the top hotels Waterford has to offer.

The Park Hotel, Dungarvan

The Park Hotel, Dungarvan is situated on five acres of landscaped gardens, overlooking the attractive Colligan River Estuary and just five minutes from the harbour town of Dungarvan.

Within easy reach of the Clonea Strand blue flag beach, excellent golf courses and the Comeragh Mountains a much loved walk for hillwalkers. This hotel offers the visitor luxury 3 star hotel accommodation and is the ideal base for your next family break or activity holiday.

This Waterford gem, a favourite with both locals and visitors from afar also offers fifteen 4 star standard self catering holiday homes, excellent dining choices and a leisure centre with 20m swimming pool.

Majestic Hotel

Welcome to the 4 star Majestic Hotel located in the sunny south east of Ireland which overlooks Tramore Bay with its impressive 5km stretch of golden beach and sand dunes and is less than 10km from the historical city of Waterford.

Situated just a short stroll from the Tramore Blue Flag beach, the 60 bedroom Majestic Hotel is the perfect location for your holiday break or when visiting Waterford and touring the beautiful south east. Guests can relax and spend their free time walking, surfing, swimming and sunbathing whilst taking in the stunning coastal scenery of Tramore Bay and Brownstown Head.

The hotel enjoys an excellent reputation for weddings, widely regarded as one of the most popular venues in county Waterford and is known for its high standard of customer service and dedicated team.

Extensive free car parking is provide for all guests including complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.
