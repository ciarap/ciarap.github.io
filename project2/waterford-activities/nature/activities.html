Coastal Activities

Surfing

Along Waterford's coast, in towns such as Dunmore or Tramore, there is an abundance of surf centres.
Many of the surf clubs offer lessons as well as equipment hire. Most of the clubs offer other activities as well such as Nordic and eco walks and land yachting.
All prices are very reasonable, offering fun-filled activities for as little as €10!

Dunmore East Adventure Centre

Dunmore East Adventure Centre provides activities for adult groups such as stag and hen parties, corporate outings, camps for children and programmes for school kids. All visitors are guaranteed an action packed day out over land or in the sea.
The activities range from kayaking, sailing, windsurfing, powerboating, canoeing, and rockclimbing. Team challenges and games can also be organised. There are many adventure packages available for adult groups which can be tailored to the level of fitness of the group.

From ambitious beginners to adventurous experts, there are multi-activity programmes offered to suit your group. Team Building programmes are available to help your company find your true potential. The programmes are designed to develop skills in leadership, organisation, reasoning, communication, confidence, achieving targets and setting goals.
The kids' camps are designed to introduce children from the ages of 5 to 17 to the great outdoors through fun and games. There are activities such as treasure boat trips, archery, climbing, a variety of water based games and much more. Dunmore East Adventure Centre also provide action packed days out for National School Tours with land and water based outdoor activities specifically designed for your school.

Angling

The coastline offers many opportunities for:

  • shore fishing for bass, flounder etc. at Kilfarrasy, Bunmahon and Stradbally;
  • rock fishing from Dunabrattin head and Kilmurrin;
  • charter boats from Dungarvan and Dunmore East;
  • fly fishing days on Carrickavantry, Ballyscanlon Lakes and Knockaderry Reservoir;
  • fishing course on Ballyshunnock Reservoir- permits from Centra Supermarket, Kilmeaden on the N25;
